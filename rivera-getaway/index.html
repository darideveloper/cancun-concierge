TULUM - TANKAH EXPEDITION

Let the adventure begin! We take you to the archaeological site of Tulum. Here you'll take a one-hour guided tour followed by 30 minutes free to take pictures and enjoy the wonderful views of this ancient city; the only one built by the Mayas on a cliff by the beautiful Caribbean Sea.

Then we'll transfer to Tankah Park, a nature sanctuary where you can swim in four different crystalline cenotes, jump from three zip lines, paddle a native canoe, meet an authentic Maya community or just relax in the hammock area. Before returning to your hotel, you'll enjoy a delicious local buffet..

CENOTES & PARADISE LAGOON

Navigate through breathtaking caves and caverns and discover the ancient rock formations up close! Next, snorkel through a fantastic inlet where saltwater and freshwater mix creating the ideal habitat for a multitude of sea life.

XEL-HA PARK

Spend a day at Xel-Há All Inclusive! Have a great time being in touch with nature at this beautiful park in Riviera Maya. Walk by jungle trails, visit the Nursery with more than 270 native species of plants, and relax for a while on an inner tube for one or two guests as the river flows into an enormous inlet.

Xel-Há All-Inclusive is an astounding place, one of the best Riviera Maya parks where you will have unlimited snorkel, food, and beverages during your visit. Explore the inlet and spend great moments jumping off the Cliff of Courage or gliding on a zip-line that ends in the water.

At Xel-Há park, you must go to the top of the Scenic Lighthouse to have an outstanding view at 131 ft tall, to then descend on a waterslide that ends on the refreshing waters of a natural pool. Discover more than 20 attractions and enjoy an unforgettable day in here.

If you visit Xel-Há from Cancun or Playa del Carmen, you will spend a day surrounded by nature and having food and beverages included with your admission. The Xel-Há gastronomy will delight you since the park has four buffet restaurants with international options, and one dedicated to Mexican food and Yucatan specialties. Also, there are two bars to refresh yourself and taste one of their famous cocktails.

Come with your family and spend a day at this all-inclusive park in Riviera Maya!

Inclusions

  • Unlimited snorkeling, fins and snorkel mask
  • River floating on inner tubes
  • Scenic Lighthouse with 360° views from a height of 131 ft and a fun descent
  • Continental breakfast from 9:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m.
  • Snacks and lunch buffets from 11:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m.
  • Domestic open bar and unlimited non-alcoholic beverages
  • World of Adventure: zip-lines, five-meter-high jumps and different rope games
  • Hikes through jungle paths
  • Cenotes and coves
  • The Meliponary and Nursery
  • Life jackets, inner tubes and bicycles
  • Restrooms, changing rooms, showers, lockers and a towel

Itinerary suggested

  • 9:00 a.m. Hotel departure
  • 9:30 a.m. Arrival to Xel-Ha
  • 9:15 a.m. Enjoy park facilities, food & beverages
  • 4:00 p.m. Departure from the park
  • 4:30 p.m. Estimated arrival to the hote

Splash into a refreshing cenote on board of an original hammock zip-line. Paddle through underground caverns in a unique raft. Besides, be one of the first to try Xplor’s new activity in a cavernous adventure at: Underground Expedition. Recover energy in two smoothie and snack stations. Satisfy your hunger in a cavemen-worthy buffet. However you want. As many times as you want.

With your Xplor admission you’ll have access to every activity at Xplor and the complete freedom to live them in any way you decide. Jump from every zipline as many times as you want, or have your dessert before your vegetables... at Xplor, you’ll be free to decide how to live the adventure.
