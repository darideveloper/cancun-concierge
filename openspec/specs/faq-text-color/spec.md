## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: FAQ answer text uses dark beige

FAQ answer paragraphs within `.faqs .questions-wrapper .question` SHALL use `#c9ad85` (dark beige) for readable contrast on white backgrounds.

#### Scenario: FAQ paragraph answers use dark beige
- **WHEN** a FAQ answer paragraph renders inside `.faqs .questions-wrapper .question`
- **THEN** its color SHALL be `#c9ad85`
