WHALE WATCHING IN PUERTO VALLARTA

Witness the awe-inspiring enormity and grace of 40ft (12m) humpback whales up-close, in their natural environment. Encounter these creatures from December to March during their winter migration. Humpbacks, renowned for their agility, engage in breaching, lob-tailing and tail-slapping.

They come to Vallarta during this season for mating and calving. Board our inflatable speed boats along with our expert marine guide to fully appreciate this fascinating species. We are the only company to use a light aircraft to track the whales, so you have the best opportunity to see the humpbacks at play.
