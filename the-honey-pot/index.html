CENOTES & PARADISE LAGOON

Jump in and float along on a guided tour in the brisk waters of ancient Mayan cenotes. Cenotes are natural sinkholes that expose groundwater underneath. Navigate through breathtaking caves and caverns and discover the ancient rock formations up close! Next, snorkel through a fantastic inlet where saltwater and freshwater mix creating the ideal habitat for a multitude of sea life.

Inclusions

  • Shared RT transportation
  • Snack on board the vans (bottled water, fruit and crackers)
  • Snorkeling gear
  • Gratuities
  • Taxes

Itinerary

  • Hotel departure: 7:00 a.m
  • Estimated return to the HOTEL: 2:00 p.m

What to bring

  • Bathing suit under clothing, comfortable footwear
  • Biodegradable sunscreen and repellent
  • Sunglasses, beach towel, a change of clothes
  • Underwater camera
  • Money for souvenirs

GUARANTEES & CAPACITIES

  • Minimum for private: 10 participants
  • Maximum capacity: 20 participants

NOTES

  • Guests must be relatively fit and must be able to swim
  • We do not recommend participation if you have limited mobility, pre-existing respiratory/heart conditions or conditions causing loss of consciousness
  • Minimum guarantee required for a private tour is 10 participants
  • You are about to book a public tour, which means you will likely share the excursion with other guests or tourists. However, Cancun Concierge DMC will closely monitor reservations, and once the required minimum for a private tour is met, we will seamlessly transition to an exclusive tour for The Honey Pot group
  • Rest assured, this change will not affect the hotel departure and arrival times, nor the price of the tour
