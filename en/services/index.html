Services

Airport Transfers

Professional and friendly staff. Luxurious vehicles and excellent service.

Aquatic Activities

Cancun offers a wide variety of aquatic activities, from luxury yachts to wave runners. Cancun has it all, fishing, snorkeling, sailing, diving, island tours etc. You only need to ask and we will provide you with the best possible option.

Excursions

Due to its privileged location, Cancun gives you the opportunity to explore the Mayan Jungle and discover the surrounding area. Archaeological sites, natural wells, parks, museums, Mexican markets, shopping malls, beautiful Carribean towns. Cancun almost has it all!

Golf

For many people, golf is an essential part of a great vacation. Cancun and the surrounding areas offer you 15 golf courses and has become a world-class golfing resort for golf fans and professionals alike. This is due to some of the unique golf courses which have been designed by leading architects.

Amenities

If you would like to offer your guests a gift, we can offer you a wide range of artisan and designed Mexican products. We will work with you to provide options suited to your preferences and your budget.

Restaurants

Cancun offers a wide variety of restaurants for all tastes and budgets. From elegant restaurants in the hotel area to traditional delicacies in the center, the options are infinite. Seafood, Mexica, Yucatan, Meats, Hamburgers, Kosher, Vegan and so much more. You’ll never be hungry on this side of the border!

Entertainment

Excellent entertainment for all tastes and occasions, our flexible options adapt to meet your budget. From Mariachi trios to Salsa, to Rock and Roll, our wide range of musicians cover all musical genres.

Decoration

Cancun and the surrounding area offers a wide range of venues to hold your dream event. The beautiful turquoise blue of the Caribbean Sea acts as a backdrop which embellishes this spectacular location. Add some small decorative ideas and your event will be unforgettable.

Floral

Whatever the occasion, floral details are indispensable. Our flowers give life to, and embellish our other decorative services.

Team Building

How do we do Team Building?
Through fun, short-term integration activities, inside or outside your hotel, from 10 to 3500 people. We have more than thirty activities that can be customize according to the level of physical activity required, schedule, theme, exteriors or interiors and budget.
