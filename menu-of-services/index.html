Airport Transfers

Professional and friendly staff, deluxe vehicles, superb service.

Our staff will be waiting for all your guests just outside the airport terminal in front to the main exit doors holding a sign with the name and logo of your company. As we contact your guests, we will escort them to the awaiting vehicles, immediately after, your guests will be transfer to your resort.

VEHICLE OPTIONS

  • Deluxe SUVs (4 passengers)
  • Vans (8 passengers)
  • Sprinters (14 passengers)
  • Buses (50 passengers)

OUR SERVICES INCLUDE

  • One-way private transportation
  • Meet & greet staff
  • Airport signage
  • Bottled waters
  • Fresh facial towels
  • Driver gratuities
  • Airport taxes

Catamaran & Reef Snorkel Expedition

Come and enjoy a wonderful sailing experience in the turquoise waters of the Mexican Caribbean. Snorkel in the second largest reef in the world. Admire the beauty of the coral formations and colorful fish, definitely one of the best ways to see the amazing underwater world.

Itinerary suggested

  • 9:00 a.m. Hotel departure
  • 9:25 a.m. Arrival to the marina
  • 9:30 a.m. Departure from the dock Sailing & snorkelin
  • 1:30 p.m. Return to the marina
  • 2:00 p.m. Estimated return to the hotel

Inclusions

  • RT transportation
  • Private catamaran sail
  • Snorkeling gear
  • Premium open bar
  • Snacks
  • Docking fee
  • Gratuities

What to bring

  • Biodegradable sunscreen
  • Change of clothes
  • Sunglasses
  • Swimsuit
  • Towels
  • Extra cash for souvenirs (optional)

Notes

This tour is not suitable for pregnant women, or persons with back problems
